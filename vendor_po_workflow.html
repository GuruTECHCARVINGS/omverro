Purchase Order Workflow

Understanding the PO Process for Vendors

How Purchase Orders Work

Vendor's Role in PO Process

1

Submit RFQ Response

You submit your quotation for buyer's requirements

2

Receive Award

Buyer awards the contract to you

3

Accept Award

You confirm acceptance of terms and conditions

4

Buyer Creates PO

Buyer generates official Purchase Order

5

Acknowledge PO

You acknowledge receipt and confirm delivery details

What You Can Do

✅ Vendor Actions

  • • Accept or decline awarded RFQs
  • • Acknowledge received POs
  • • Update delivery schedules
  • • Request PO modifications
  • • Track PO status
  • • Submit delivery confirmations

❌ Buyer Actions Only

  • • Create Purchase Orders
  • • Modify PO terms
  • • Cancel Purchase Orders
  • • Release PO for processing
