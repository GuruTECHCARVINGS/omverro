Settings

User Profile & Access Control

  • Company profile info (name, contact, tax details)
  • Users & roles (admin, viewer, approver)
  • Access rights and data visibility settings

Dashboard Layout & Widgets

  • Customizable widgets (e.g., open POs, pending invoices, payment status)
  • Filter/sort options for data views (date ranges, document types)
  • Chart & KPI displays (delivery performance, invoice aging, etc.)

Notifications & Alerts

  • Email/SMS notifications settings for:
    • New PO received
    • Invoice approval/rejection
    • Payment status updates
  • Frequency and type of alerts

Document Management Settings

  • Upload/download templates for invoices, ASN (Advance Shipping Notice), etc.
  • Preferred document formats (PDF, XML, Excel)
  • OCR preferences if applicable (for scanned invoice processing)

Support & Feedback Options

  • Contact support form or chatbot access
  • Option to raise tickets or feedback from within the dashboard

Localization Settings

  • Language and currency preferences
  • Time zone settings
